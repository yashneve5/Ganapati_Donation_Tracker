About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the dedicated team behind Ganpati Tracker

Our Mission

To celebrate Lord Ganpati with utmost devotion, transparency, and community participation while maintaining complete accountability of all donations and expenses. We strive to create a platform that brings together devotees from all walks of life to honor Vighnaharta Ganpati.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and transparent platform for Ganpati Utsav celebrations, fostering community spirit and devotion while setting new standards for accountability in religious and cultural events. We envision a future where every devotee can contribute with complete confidence.

About Lord Ganpati

Lord Ganpati, also known as Ganesha, is the beloved elephant-headed deity who is revered as the remover of obstacles and the patron of arts and sciences. He is traditionally invoked before beginning any new venture, making him the perfect deity to guide our community celebrations.

Our annual Ganpati festival is not just a religious celebration but a cultural phenomenon that brings together people from all backgrounds, fostering unity, devotion, and community spirit. Through this platform, we aim to preserve these traditions while embracing modern transparency and accountability.

Festival Highlights

  • 10-day grand celebration from Ganesh Chaturthi to Anant Chaturdashi
  • Daily cultural programs including music, dance, and drama
  • Community service initiatives and charity drives
  • Eco-friendly celebrations with clay idols and natural decorations
  • Inclusive participation from all age groups and communities

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted community platform, here's our story of growth and dedication.

Digital Transformation
